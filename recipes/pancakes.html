Pancakes

Description

Who doesn't want to start their day with pancakes? Or end it!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbs white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbs melted butter

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients together (flour, baking powder, salt, sugar)
  2. Make a whole in the middle of the bowl, and pour the rest of the ingredients (milk, egg, butter). Mix well
  3. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan (or griddle), and pour about 1/4 of pancake batter for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot

Tip!

If you want to save some pancakes for later, you can always refrigerate them and reheat in a 350 degree F oven for 5-7 minutes.
