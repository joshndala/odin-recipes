Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

I can never get enough of chocolate chip cookies, and neither can my friends when I bake for them! Making them from scratch means you'll get a fresh batch when you want, and the dough can be frozen for when you want to bake more later.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F, and set aside a baking pan with parchment paper
  2. In one bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt
  3. In another bowl, mix the butter and sugars until creamy. Then mix in the eggs and vanilla
  4. Add the dry ingredients mixture to the creamy bowl and mix
  5. Add the chocolate chips and mix
  6. Roll 2-3 tbs (or however big you want your cookies to be) of dough into balls and place them evenly spaced on the baking pan
  7. Bake in the oven for approximately 8-10 minutes
  8. Set aside for 2 minutes before moving them onto a cooling rack
